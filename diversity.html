Diversity

As the world becomes increasingly diverse, it is crucial that K-State students are educated on how to be appreciative of diversity and how to make their communities, at K-State and beyond, welcoming and inclusive. If we as members of this university community truly advocate for a "K-State Family" atmosphere, then we need to hold each other accountable to standards that do not tolerate discrimination and which take meaningful steps to prevent a campus climate which lacks inclusion.

The Van Ranken-Kennedy campaign would like to push for these steps by taking an active role in the implementation of cultural competency training for faculty, since they are charged with developing and cultivating the intellectual environment present at K-State.
