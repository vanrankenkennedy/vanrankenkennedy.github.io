Mental Wellness

Student wellness is a critical component to student success. The Van Ranken-Kennedy campaign recognizes that it is crucial to the success of the K-State student body to understand that student health is both physical and mental, and that both of those aspects of health should be equally served and provided. Unfortunately in 2016, there still remains a stigma against mental wellness.

The Van Ranken-Kennedy campaigns values the mental wellness services provided across the K-State campus and believes that a greater partnership can be established between Counseling Services and SGA to amplify the positive effects these important services have on the mental wellness of the campus. The Van Ranken-Kennedy campaign supports and will work towards expansion of Counseling Services outreach to help reach more students across the campus community. Specifically, a peer education program will be prioritized to enable a greater form of outreach across campus in a relatable manner while saving critical and limited clinical staff's time and allowing them to increase time spent with individual patients.
