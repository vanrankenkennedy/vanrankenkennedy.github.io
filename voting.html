Political Advocacy

The Van Ranken-Kennedy campaign is seeking to address the biggest problem facing college students, and that is the rising cost of tuition and the price of an increasingly valuable education. Jessica and Trenton aim to create a political advocacy organization called Kansas Student Voice, with the goal of organizing students at higher education institutions in the state of Kansas to influence the Kansas legislature to make higher education a greater priority.

Kansas Student Voice would create a grassroots effort to make students not a special interest of the political system in Kansas, but rather a chief interest. They will use unique strategies to engage students at K-State and beyond to carry out a campaign for efficient student advocacy for higher education.
